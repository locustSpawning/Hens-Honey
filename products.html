Welcome to Hens & Honey Shoppe where you’ll find luxurious & deliciously handcrafted natural soap, soap products, and other quirky household items. We offer over 65 unique skin loving handmade soaps that will drench your skin with a velvety intoxication that is irresistible! Stop by to grab a ready made gift basket or pick out items you want, and while you wait, we’ll customize one for you. Hens & Honey Shoppe also makes soap favors for weddings, bridal showers, baby showers, communions, or any special event you need them for. We will customize each one individually to make them uniquely yours. Stop by our boutique shoppe, located in the historic town of Swedesboro, for a truly aromatic experience.
